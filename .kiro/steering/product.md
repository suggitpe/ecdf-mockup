# Product Overview

## Product Name
Career Development Assessment Website

## Purpose
A standalone web application that enables employees to evaluate and track their career growth through structured assessments based on a 9-pillar Employee Career Development Framework (ECDF).

## Target Users

### Primary Users
1. **Employees**: Create evidence of their work, perform self-assessments, track progress, and manage personal development plans
2. **Managers**: Review and independently assess employee evidence, track team progress, and identify development needs
3. **Independent Assessors**: Provide unbiased third-party evaluations of employee evidence

## Key Features

### Core Assessment Framework
- **9-Pillar Framework**: Thinks, Engages, Influences, Achieves, Defines, Designs, Delivers, Controls, Operates
- **Evidence-Based Assessment**: All assessments based on documented work activities
- **Multi-Role Independent Assessment**: Employees, Managers, and Independent Assessors score evidence independently
- **Scoring System**: Each pillar scored 1-5 based on evidence quality

### Evidence Management
- **Evidence Creation**: Employees document work activities with three quality factors:
  1. Impact (effect on the firm)
  2. Complexity (difficulty and scope)
  3. Personal Contribution (individual's specific role)
- **Multi-Assessor Scoring**: Each evidence item independently assessed by Employee, Manager, and Independent Assessor
- **Evidence Versioning**: Point-in-time snapshots preserve assessment history

### ECDF Assessments
- **Score Aggregation**: Overall pillar scores calculated by aggregating evidence scores across all evidence items
- **Assessment Versioning**: Multiple assessments over time as employees accumulate evidence
- **Temporal Tracking**: Track career development progress across assessment versions
- **Draft Saving**: Save assessment progress without submitting

### Performance Evaluation
- **Grade-Based Expectations**: Expected scores configured for each employee grade and pillar
- **Performance Status**: Identify under-performing, at-expectation, and over-performing pillars
- **Personal Development Plans**: Create and track development goals for under-performing areas
- **Progress Visualization**: Charts and graphs showing growth over time

### Manager Features
- **Team Dashboard**: Overview of all assigned employees
- **Individual Progress Tracking**: View employee development over time
- **Team Analytics**: Aggregate statistics, performance distribution, and trend analysis
- **Pending Evidence Review**: Identify evidence requiring manager assessment

## Business Goals

1. **Structured Career Development**: Provide a systematic framework for evaluating and tracking employee competencies
2. **Evidence-Based Evaluation**: Ground assessments in concrete work activities rather than subjective opinions
3. **Multi-Perspective Assessment**: Combine self-assessment with manager and independent assessor perspectives
4. **Growth Tracking**: Enable employees and managers to track competency development over time
5. **Targeted Development**: Identify skill gaps and create actionable development plans
6. **Team Management**: Give managers visibility into team competencies and development needs

## Success Metrics

- Employee engagement with evidence creation and self-assessment
- Completion rate of multi-role assessments (Employee, Manager, Independent Assessor)
- Number of ECDF assessments completed over time
- Development plan creation and completion rates
- Improvement in pillar scores over time
- Manager utilization of team analytics and progress tracking

## Platform
- **Web-Only Interface**: Optimized for desktop and laptop browsers
- **No Mobile App**: Focus on desktop/laptop experience only
